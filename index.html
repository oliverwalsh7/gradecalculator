Step One
Obtain your unofficial academic transcript
Step Two
Retrieve your total gpa hours & total quality points. Total gpa hours are only credit hours that have gone towards your gpa and exclude credits from things such as AP classes, failed classes, and transfer credits. Total quality points is the value of a grade (ex. A = 4.0) times the credit hours of that course for every course you have taken.
Step Three
Input your hypothetical / projected semesterly grades in the input section. The keep grade checkbox will exclude a class from the pass/fail optimization if you know you want to keep the course for a grade. The retake calculation is done as a grade replacement.
DISCLAIMER
This app is simply meant to be a tool for calculating one's gpa. It is not meant to be the ultimate dictator of any decisions made about one's academic career. No personal data is being collected or sent about any users.

The Grade Wizard Algorithm
The Grade Wizard was implemented in order to automate the process of determining which classes a student should count for credit in order to optimize their cumulative GPA.
Through the use of a magical algorithm, The Grade Wizard deduces which classes will positively impact one's cumulative GPA, through the use of quality points and GPA hours.
We also provide a course grade calculator as a supplemental tool that one can use to project their grade in a specific class.
